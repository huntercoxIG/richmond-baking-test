Creating Raving Fan Customers

At Richmond Baking, our goal is create raving fan customers-this means dedicating ourselves to exceeding the expectations of our customers at every turn. We know that the key to creating lifelong customers lies in not only meeting your needs but anticipating your wants and creating unique custom solutions every time. As a Contract Manufacturer, we understand that we are not only working for you, but working for your customers, as well.

Our Customer Service and Sales team has a wide range of industry experience making them the perfect people to represent Richmond Baking in earning your business and your trust. Our sales staff has many years of industry-specific experience and has worked in our customers' shoes-formulating, processing, assuring quality in production and packaging for a broad range of ingredients and finished food products. Combine this knowledge of industry nuances with a commitment to customer service, and you have a recipe for success.
