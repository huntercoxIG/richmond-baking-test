Since its inception in 1902, Richmond Baking has been led by four generations of the Quigg family. The Executive Team is comprised of hands-on leaders who are involved in all of the day-to-day operations and decisions at Richmond Baking. Under the leadership of the Quigg family, Richmond Baking has become a stable, growth-oriented company that serves a broad spectrum of the food industry. Thanks to the corporate atmosphere of integrity, respect and Family Pride, Richmond Baking boasts a number of long-term employees (not just Quiggs) who contribute to the success of the company.
